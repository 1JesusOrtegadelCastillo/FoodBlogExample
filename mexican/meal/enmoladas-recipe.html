    Chicken Enmoladas

    Ingredients

    - Chicken breast

    - Corn tortillas

    - Mole sauce

    - Purple onion

    - Olive oil

    - Milk cream

    Preparation

    *Quantity of ingredients will depends on how much people are going to eat*

    step 1: Let's start boiling the chicken breast with half onion and 2 peeled garlic cloves and shred chicken once is cooked.

    step 2: Empty the mole sauce in a deep pan until it boil.

    step 3: Make sure you can roll the corn tortillas, if not, warm them in a microwave.

    step 4: Having chicken breast and mole sauce ready, now you just need to prepare a pan with enough olive oil, put some chicken in a corn tortilla and roll.

    step 5: Once you make the rolls, put them in the pan with the hot olive oil and fry.

    step 6: Now, fried the enmolada rolls, put them on a plate and add enough mole sauce

    step 7: You can put some chopped onion, milk cream, cheese, avocado and lettuce on top and enjoy!

    Chicken enmoladas is a typical food in Mexico full of flavor thanks to the mole sauce and the ingredients you can add to it like, lettuce, onion and avocado.
