    Mexican "Pork" Beans

    Ingredients

    - 1 Kilogram of Pinto Beans

    - 2 and a half liters of water

    - 1 soup spoon of lard (optional, but it really change for good the flavor)

    - 1 soup spoon of salt

    - 1 soup spoon of red hot chili powder

    - Melty Cheese (optional)

    Preparation

    *Quantity of ingredients will depends on how much people are going to eat*

    First make sure: You always clean correctly your beans (sometimes it comes with small rocks and stuff). Once you are sure they are clean, soak beans on cold water just for make sure they do not have any kind of impurities.

    step 1: Boil 1 kilogram of pinto beans with 2 and half liters of water and 1 soup spoon of salt for approx 2 hours with low fire.

    - Sometimes water may evaporate fast so make sure you check them and add more water if needed.

    You will notice beans are ready when they are soft enough to grind them.

    step 2: Once beans are ready, grind them with a hand blender.

    step 3: Take a soup spoon of lard (if you decided to use it, if not, you can use olive oil) and warm it in a deep pan (low fire) until it melt.

    step 4: When lard is melt, add 1 soup spoon of red chili powder and make a quick mix and remove from fire

    step 5: In another deep pan, put the grinded beans, warm them in low fire and add the mix we did with lard and red chili powder, if you decided to add cheese this is the moment and after that just mix again.

    ENJOY !!

    Extra tip: Sometimes beans in general gets dry after keeping them in a refrigerator, if that happens to you, just add a little bit of water and they will be ready again.
