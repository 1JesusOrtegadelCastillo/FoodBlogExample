    Chicken Chilaquiles

    Ingredients

    - Olive oil

    - Corn Tortillas or tortilla chips

    - red chili sauce

    - Chicken breast

    - Garlic

    - Onion

    - Cilantro/Coriander

    - Mexican cheese or grated cheese

    Preparation

    *Quantity of ingredients will depends on how much people are going to eat*

    step 1: Check Mexican Red chili sauce recipe.

    step 2: Boil chicken breast with half onion and two peeled garlic cloves. Once is ready, shred the chicken.

    step 3: Cut corn tortillas in medium size squares or triangles (if you are using tortilla chips go to step 5).

    step 4: Put enough olive / spray oil in a skillet and fry corn tortillas.

    step 5: On a warm skillet, put fried tortillas / tortilla chips and add the red chili sauce.

    (If you are using grated cheese, put it in too)

    step 6: Once everything is warm, add the shredded chicken breast and mix it all.

    step 7: serve it on a plate, chop some fresh onion, cilantro/coriander and add some mexican cheese.

    step 8: Enjoy! (you can add some eggs or beans next to your chilaquiles)

    Chilaquiles are one of the most common breakfasts in Mexico
